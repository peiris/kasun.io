Ever wondered how to set an NFT image as your Twitter profile picture? Well, it's pretty straightforward. Here's how to do it.

## Pre-requisites

<List>
  <ListItem>$10 worth of Etherium OR $10 to buy Etherium.</ListItem>
  <ListItem>
    [Coinbase Wallet App](https://www.coinbase.com/wallet/downloads) installed on your mobile with an Etherium wallet.
  </ListItem>
  <ListItem>
    An OpenSea account with a connected Coinbase wallet.
    [OpenSea.io](https://opensea.io/)
  </ListItem>
  <ListItem>A Binance account.</ListItem>
</List>
